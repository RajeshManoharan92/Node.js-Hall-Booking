	'/hall/getroomdetails' 			-> For getting room details,
	'/hall/getcustomerdata' 		-> For getting customer data, (List all customers with booked data)
	'/hall/getbookingstatus' 		-> For getting booking status (List all rooms with booked data).
	'/hall/postcustomerdetails'              -> For Booking a room
	'/hall/updatecustomerdetails/:id'	-> For updating customer details
	'/hall/deletecustomerdetails/:id'	-> For deleting customer
